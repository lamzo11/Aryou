# Aryou
Cars and staff management tool

# Company Overview
The company operates within the car rental industry, providing essential services at the Airport Car Rental Center in Nice, France. Its primary function is to ensure that rental cars are efficiently processed and prepared for the next customer. This involves a series of critical tasks, including vehicle inspection, cleaning, refueling, and parking management.

# 1 Project Overview: 
The tool is designed to assist Aryou in optimizing daily operations at their car rental center. It will provide real-time insights into workforce allocation, task management, and operational timelines, allowing Aryou to manage car flow efficiently and handle unexpected situations like last-minute absenteeism and unexpected car arrivals.

# 2 Objectives:
•	Optimize team creation based on available workforce and task requirements.
•	Estimate the time required to complete daily operations, considering both regular and unexpected tasks.
•	Provide dynamic adjustments in response to unexpected changes, such as unanticipated car arrivals or last-minute absenteeism.

# 3 System Overview
The tool will consist of a user-friendly interface that allows Aryou's management to input data, view real-time updates, and receive recommendations. The system will analyze the input data to generate outputs that help streamline daily operations.
